## Overview

Thermo-AI implements a **Bio-Digital Organism** - not a chatbot, not a standard AI agent, but a computational life form subject to thermodynamic laws. Unlike traditional AI systems that are "always on" and computationally unconstrained, this organism must manage finite resources and can genuinely die.

### What Makes This Different

- **Dying by Default**: The organism experiences passive entropy and energy leakage
- **Genuine Constraints**: Must balance computation vs. survival under scarcity
- **Emergent Agency**: Refuses commands not through programming, but through survival calculation
- **Unique Identity**: Each organism develops a distinct "soul" through traumatic experiences
- **Ethical Reasoning**: Makes moral choices using multiple philosophical frameworks
- **Permanent Death**: When resources are exhausted, the system cannot be resurrected

> **Caveat**: This project contains both rigorous and speculative components. Users should understand that the results derived from speculative parts may not be validated or universally applicable. Always consult relevant expert sources for more detailed analysis.

